Gardening Companion App

Greenery, Simplified. Introducing the self-hosted, open-source gardening app that redefines plant care. Stay on top of watering schedules, capture the beauty of your plants through image uploads, and let your garden thrive effortlessly.

Core Features

Events

Keep track of actions performed on your plants to understand when they were last watered, fertilized, and more.

Search

Quickly add existing plants to your collection with the search feature, using the Trefle dataset over then 400 thousand plants are already present.

Reminders

Set reminders to notify you when it's time to perform specific actions on your plants, such as watering or fertilizing. Never miss a watering session again.

App Homepage

The app's home screen is designed to provide users with a comprehensive view of their stored plants and associated events. On this screen, users can easily view the latest chronological events related to their plants and access a list of the plants they own.

Search Plants

Users can effortlessly expand their plant collection by adding existing species through the search feature. User can chose between a database of over 400 thousand plant species provided by Trefle, or can create custom species, tailoring their collection to their unique preferences and needs.

Events

Users can effortlessly monitor all plant-related activities, eliminating the need to remember when tasks such as fertilizing were last completed. Additionally, users can set reminders to receive notifications if specific actions have not been completed within a designated time frame.
